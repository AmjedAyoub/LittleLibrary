The Little Free Library "Little Helper"

This app will bring you closer to your local micro-neighborhood while guiding you to your next read. This app utilizes the wonderful community that the Little Free Library has galvanized and takes it a step further by allowing you access to a list of books in your local 'Library,' as well as opening discussion and reviews of books tied to your local library specifically.

We'll never share your email with anyone else.
